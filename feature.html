Savathûn, Sword-Breaker, and Sister of Shapes. Savathûn, Sword-Breaker, and Sister of Shapes. Those who are determined to comprehend the Demon Queen's mysterious hold over the Light are awaited. DETAILS

Savathûn's Throne World is a twisted wonderland where a precarious balance of power exists. Everything she hides is here, from the glittering palace to the murky swamp.

Learn how to use the new crafting system to make unique bespoke weapons using complex stat pools, mods, and shaders. Never take a chance when battling a deity.

At the Relic, a brand-new weapon typology sets out on its adventure. Master your Glaive to perform potent melee combos, launch projectiles, and deploy a potent energy shield once it has been fine-tuned to perfection.

With Stasis, a new elemental subclass with roots in Darkness, Explorers who transform into Shadebinders, Revenants, or Behemoths will learn how to control this extraordinary ability to rule the battlefield.
